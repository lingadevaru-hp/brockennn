# Good Bond Trips

## Summary
A travel startup co-founded by Thoshan and Tejas, based in Kunigal, Karnataka. Live website at [goodbondtrips.com](https://goodbondtrips.com). Offers curated group travel experiences.

**Tagline:** *Travel Together, Bond Forever*

## Status
**Active** — live and operating.

## Business Details

- Co-founded by Thoshan and Tejas
- Based in Kunigal, Karnataka
- Minimum 10 members required for any trip to depart
- T&C applied on every trip

## Live

- **Website:** [goodbondtrips.com](https://goodbondtrips.com)

## Active Tracks

- **SEO Optimization:** Ongoing efforts to improve search visibility.
- **Mobile App Development:** Expo / React Native (WebView wrapper) — planned.
- **Operations:** Customer-facing PDF itinerary documents for each trip.

## Current Trip Packages

### Gokarna Beach Trek
**Inclusions:** Non-AC transportation, 2 Breakfasts & 1 Dinner, Professional Guide, Accommodation (2 or 3 sharing).  
**Exclusions:** Personal expenses, entry fees, anything not in inclusions.

### Tadiandamol Trek
**Inclusions:** Non-AC transportation, food (as per itinerary), Professional Guide, Accommodation (2 or 3 sharing), Trek Permit.  
**Exclusions:** Day 2 Lunch & Dinner, personal expenses, anything not in inclusions.

### Mantralaya Trip
**Inclusions:** Non-AC transportation, Professional Guide, Accommodation (2 or 3 sharing).  
**Exclusions:** Food, entry fees, personal expenses, anything not in inclusions.

## Group Policy

- Group size: 10+ members minimum for departure.
- Standard T&C applies on every trip.

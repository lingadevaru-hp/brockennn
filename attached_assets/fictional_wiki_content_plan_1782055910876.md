# Fictional Wikipedia Content Plan

## Overview
This document outlines the fictional content for a Wikipedia clone, focusing on a modern design and interlinked articles. The theme will be a whimsical, slightly anachronistic world with elements of fantasy and technology.

## Core Concepts & Universe
*   **World Name:** Aethelgard
*   **Primary Focus:** The history, geography, notable figures, and unique technologies of Aethelgard.
*   **Key Factions/Societies:**
    *   The Lumina Collective (advanced, ethereal beings)
    *   The Ironclad Dominion (industrial, militaristic empire)
    *   The Verdant Weavers (nature-aligned, magical practitioners)
    *   The Sky-Merchants Guild (traders and explorers with airships)

## Initial Article Ideas (Interlinked)

### 1. Aethelgard (Main Page/Portal)
*   **Summary:** An introduction to the world, its major continents, and a brief history.
*   **Links to:** Lumina Collective, Ironclad Dominion, Verdant Weavers, Sky-Merchants Guild, Great Aetheric Rift, Floating Cities of Zephyr.

### 2. The Lumina Collective
*   **Summary:** Details on their philosophy, technology (e.g., Aether-Crystals), and social structure.
*   **Links to:** Aethelgard, Aether-Crystals, Lumina Spires, The Great Harmonization.

### 3. The Ironclad Dominion
*   **Summary:** History of its rise, military might, key leaders, and industrial innovations (e.g., Steam-Golems).
*   **Links to:** Aethelgard, Steam-Golems, Obsidian Forges, The War of Gears.

### 4. The Verdant Weavers
*   **Summary:** Their connection to nature, magical practices (e.g., Life-Threads), and ancient forest sanctuaries.
*   **Links to:** Aethelgard, Life-Threads, Whispering Woods, Elderwood Council.

### 5. Sky-Merchants Guild
*   **Summary:** Their trade routes, famous airships (e.g., Cloud-Skimmers), and notable expeditions.
*   **Links to:** Aethelgard, Cloud-Skimmers, Trade Winds, Port Azure.

### 6. Aether-Crystals
*   **Summary:** A detailed explanation of their properties, uses (power source, communication), and origins.
*   **Links to:** The Lumina Collective, Great Aetheric Rift, Lumina Spires.

### 7. Steam-Golems
*   **Summary:** The engineering marvels of the Ironclad Dominion, their construction, and deployment in warfare and industry.
*   **Links to:** The Ironclad Dominion, Obsidian Forges, The War of Gears.

### 8. Life-Threads
*   **Summary:** The magical conduits used by the Verdant Weavers to manipulate nature and heal.
*   **Links to:** The Verdant Weavers, Whispering Woods, Elderwood Council.

### 9. Cloud-Skimmers
*   **Summary:** The iconic airships of the Sky-Merchants Guild, their design, propulsion, and role in trade.
*   **Links to:** Sky-Merchants Guild, Trade Winds, Port Azure.

## Design Considerations
*   **Modern UI:** Clean layout, responsive design, easy navigation.
*   **Interlinking:** Ensure all articles have relevant links to other articles within the fictional universe.
*   **Search:** A functional search bar to find articles.
*   **Categories/Tags:** Implement a system for categorizing articles.

This plan will serve as the foundation for generating the content and structuring the website.
